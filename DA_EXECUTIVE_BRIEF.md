## 💎 PROMPTING METHODOLOGY

### Prompts DA spécialisés:
Chaque image a brief ultra-précis incluant:
1. **Scene Description** - Quoi exactement visualiser
2. **Style & Cinematography** - Genre filmique, technique
3. **Color Grading** - Palette précise, tonalités
4. **Composition** - Rule of thirds, focal points, depth
5. **Technical Specs** - Résolution, aspect ratio, DOF
6. **Mood/Emotion** - Psychologie du viewer

### Exemple (Genesis Hero):
```
"Cinematic photograph: Abstract composition of volumetric 
orange light rays piercing through thick, swirling smoke 
and mist. No subjects visible, purely atmospheric. 
[STYLE]: Cinematic, high-end commercial photography...
[COLOR]: Deep blacks with warm orange highlights...
[COMPOSITION]: Leading light rays from top-left to center...
[MOOD]: Mysterious, primal, emergence..."
```

### Résultat:
- Prompts super-précis = images super-contrôlées
- 0 approximation créative
- Variations générées = choix confirmés
