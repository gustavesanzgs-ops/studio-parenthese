## 🎬 UNIFICATION GALLERY

### Palette Unifiée:
- Warm amber/gold base across all images
- Rich shadow details with slight blue tint
- Desaturated but warm overall
- Slight film stock quality (desaturation + color cast)

### Consistency Techniques:
- Same color grading LUT applied to all
- Consistent lighting philosophy (theatrical/dramatic)
- Similar DOF and focus techniques
- Unified emotional narrative progression

### Sequence Story:
1. Setup/immersion → Audience enters world
2. Connection → Characters interact
3. Detail/mystery → Narrative clues revealed
4. Environment → Scope and scale shown
5. Climax → Emotional peak reached
6. Resolution → Human connection aftermath
